Resources & Safety Info

Stretcher and Wheelchair Needs

We provide transportation for clients with special mobility needs. Stretcher rides are for patients who cannot sit upright due to medical conditions, injuries, or post-surgery care. Wheelchairs are for individuals with limited mobility, temporary injuries, or chronic conditions making walking long distances difficult. Both stretcher and wheelchair accommodations are of additional cost, ensuring safe and comfortable transportation for all.

Ride Mileage Policy

Our base rates cover standard mileage, but if your ride exceeds the planned distance for example, a scheduled ride of 30.5 miles extends to 35.5 miles-an additional charge will apply for the extra miles. This ensures fairness and accuracy for all passengers.

Multiple Drivers for Safety

We have a team of professional drivers to ensure timely, safe, and reliable service. Having multiple drivers allows us to handle overlapping requests efficiently and guarantee that a driver is always available when you need one.
